About Us

Marcos Charity is a not-for profit organization, we strive hard to provide clean and healthy drinking water for rural communities. Since 2019, Marcos Charity has been instrumental in providing clean and healthy drinking water to rural communities. We have helped 13 local communities get clean water, and we look forward to helping more local communities.
